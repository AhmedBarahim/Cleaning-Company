Corporate Social Responsibility

Actually the corporate sector in this filed has prompted to look into their social responsibility and operate sustainably Companies are under immense pressure to gain public trust and stay competitive in the global economy and are adopting the philanthropic approach of giving back to society and getting involved in social welfare and developmental activities.

Ekoclean is trying to develop business models where it can integrate ethical, social, environmental, employee and consumer concerns along with his business operations involving their stakeholders. Ekoclean is adopting and also developing his own CSR strategies, which would not just contribute to his growth plans but also towards the well being of his employees, customers, stakeholders as well as the society at large for that Ekoclean has an active CSR policy.

“We are very much in favor of promoting CSR in our field but are restricted in how much we can achieve by the low profit margin in the cleaning industry locally".

Ekoclean considers CSR as a very important part of the corporate culture They have a dedicated budget every year for research and development for Promoting Innovation in Science and Research We at Ekoclean have acquired this culture from our parent company in promoting the protection of the environment, looking after the staff and giving adequate importance to quality in our day to day operation.

CSR has a major part to play in our soft services industry and it covers the spectrum of our operations incorporating our employees, clients and their employees, and the general public with input and effect at every level.
