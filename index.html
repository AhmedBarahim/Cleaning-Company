About

Ekoclean is a company which specializes in providing the cleaning solutions and production of liquid detergents suhc as Cleaning agents, Liquid detergents, cleaning agents, Washing up liquids, All purpose cleaners, Laundry detergents, mild detergents, Fabric detergent and softening and Special cleaning agents.

Presenting a clean and comfortable environment at a cost effective price is a key challenge for today’s businesses People want a value for money service with no compromise on quality and must use their cleaning budgets wisely.

Our company Ekoclean Services, was established to meet this need We will provide Businesses or Schools with a unique set of benefits that blend quality delivery with cost saving methodologies and hands on, accessible management.
